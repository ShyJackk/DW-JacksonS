Brazilian Hair

Originated from Brazil

Most Popular, known for being very soft, very diverse, durable, thick, easiest to blend, very luxurious, lots of body, natural shine, low to medium luster.

Burmese Hair

Originated from Myanmar

Highly versatile, high luster. Very rare and exotic compared to other origins. Mix of Indian and Chinese hair, great match for those with a coarse hair texture.

Cambodian Hair

Originated from Cambodia

Slightly coarse hair, a very good match for those who have relaxed African American hairs, holds curls well, and has lots of bounce and body.

Eurasian Hair

Originated from Euro/Asia

Very similar to Brazilian hair with thickness and bouncy curls, but not as popular. A very rare hair type with a soft, smooth, silky texture.

Filipino Hair

Originated from Filipino

Thicker than Indian, Malaysian, and Brazilian hair. Easy to color and style. Not as fine as Indian hair texture, silky to medium coarseness, VERY durable.

Indian Hair

Originated from India

Versatile, very fine, super light and bouncy, curls and styles easily. Also swells and becomes quite frizzy in humid weather, anti-frizz products are recommended.

Malaysian Hair

Originated from Malaysia

Sleek and shiny, low/medium luster, holds curls well, very dark brown with ends lighter in color, blends well with all hair types, low maintenance.

Mongolian Hair

Originated from Mongolia

Mongolian hair is of an Asian origin, thicker hair shaft, not as light weight as some virgin hair, heavier density, maintains a very soft texture.

Peruvian Hair

Originated from Peru

The ultimate multi-purpose hair, very soft and light, coarser then Indian or Brazilian, low to medium luster, blends well with relaxed hair.
